4” Reverse Osmosis systems

Reverse osmosis is a proven technology to remove dissolved solids in water. Our systems are highly efficient, easy to operate & simple to maintain & offer the ideal solution for small and medium scale RO requirements.

Features

  • Membranes and pressure vessels designed to suit a specific water quality.
  • Pre-trement to ease work on the membranes.
  • 98% salt rejection rate.
  • System monitoring accesories.
  • Corrosion resistant stainless stelel high pressure pumps.
  • 0.125 - 2 cubic per hour permeate.

Solar power option is available on request

Raw or feed water quality

  • TDS of up to 2,000 ppm for the low brackish water.
  • TDS of up to 5,000 ppm for brackish water.
  • Chloride level should be less than 350 ppm.
  • No chlorine
  • Total hardness < 200 ppm
  • No suspended solids
